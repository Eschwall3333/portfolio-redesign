About Emory Schwall

I'm a man of many many interests and tastes. For starters, I've always been madly in love with music, electronic music in particular. Ever since I saw a music video for one of Daft Punk's first tracks when I was four years old, I've been hooked. Since then I've not only been obsessed with listening to music but creating and studying it as well.

When I was ten years old, I begged my parents for a guitar which is where I started learning how to play jazz. Not to long after that I got pretty bored and wanted desperately to learn how to build a guitar. A few electronics lessons from my father and tons of frustrating moments later, I built my first "franken guitar" which I still have and use to this day. My love for building guitars though soon spiraled out of control into severl different avenues like synthesizers, motorcycles, cars, audio gear, and computers.

For the past 10 years I've been building computers, synthesizers and other electronic gadgets either from salvaged parts or specific store bought parts. As a result of this hobby turned lifestyle, programming was very soon to follow in my repertoire of knowledge. I've been programming for the better part of 4 years now, mostly tinkering, but it wasn't until 2019 that I decided programming and Web development was the industry I was going to choose to build my career. I decided it was high time to further my education so I enrolled in the Georgia Institute of Technology Full Stack Web Development bootcamp. The 6 month program taught me more than I could've ever imagined and certified me as a MERN and MEAN stack web developer. Now I'm combining that knowledge with my several years of self taught skills to push my career goals in this field further and further.
